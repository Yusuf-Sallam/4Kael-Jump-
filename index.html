Standard Program

3 Specialized Tracks based on your metrics:

1. Power-Based Program ▼

  • Focus: Emphasizes power training through a predominantly weighted based program as the foundation for maximizing your vertical.
  • Ideal for: Individuals with beginner level strength and intermediate-advanced explosive ability.

2. Power/Explosiveness Program ▼

  • Focus: Combines power and explosiveness through a balanced mix of weighted and plyometric training to maximize your vertical.
  • Ideal for: Individuals with intermediate strength/explosive ability, or a relatively even mix of both

3. Explosiveness Program ▼

  • Focus: Concentrates on explosive training through a predominantly plyometric oriented program.
  • Ideal for: Individuals with advanced strength and beginner-intermediate explosive ability.
